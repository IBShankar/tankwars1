**TankWars is live and playable! 🎮 Here's the full breakdown:**

✅ What's built

**Two full game modes**:
1 Player — you vs an AI tank that tracks and hunts you down
2 Players — local multiplayer on same keyboard

**Full game mechanics:**
Destructible walls that break after 2 hits
Bullets that bounce once off walls and edges
3 HP per tank with live health bars
Score tracking across rounds
Particle explosion effects on hits
Live AI commentary that reacts to hits, misses, low health and taunts

**Controls:**
Player 1 — Arrow keys to move/rotate, Enter to shoot
Player 2 — WASD to move/rotate, Space to shoot
